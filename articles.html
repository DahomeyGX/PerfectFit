What is Fast Fashion?

Fast fashion refers to the rapid production of inexpensive clothing to keep up with the latest trends. This often leads to environmental and ethical issues, including waste and poor working conditions.

How to Repurpose Your Clothes

Repurposing clothes can extend their lifespan and reduce waste. Consider these tips:

  • Turn old jeans into a trendy denim jacket or a bag.
  • Transform worn-out t-shirts into cleaning rags or cushion covers.
  • Upcycle old sweaters into scarves or mittens.
