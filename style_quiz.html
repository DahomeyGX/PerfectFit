Style Descriptions

Casual

Comfortable and laid-back. Think jeans, t-shirts, and sneakers. This style is all about ease and practicality.

Bohemian (Boho)

A free-spirited style that often includes flowing fabrics, ethnic prints, and vintage or handcrafted items. It emphasizes individuality and creativity.

Chic

Sophisticated and elegant. This style often includes tailored pieces, neutral colors, and classic cuts. It's polished and refined.

Streetwear

Urban and trendy, this style incorporates elements like oversized hoodies, graphic tees, and sneakers. It's heavily influenced by skate and hip-hop culture.

Preppy

Inspired by the attire of Ivy League students, this style includes items like blazers, chinos, and polo shirts. It’s often associated with a clean, put-together look.

Gothic

Dark and dramatic, often featuring black clothing, leather, lace, and heavy makeup. This style is influenced by goth music and subcultures.

Vintage/Retro

Fashion inspired by past decades, whether it’s the 1920s flapper dresses, 1950s rockabilly, or 1980s bold prints. It often includes authentic vintage pieces or modern interpretations.

Minimalist

Focuses on simplicity and functionality with a preference for clean lines, neutral colors, and understated designs. It's all about less being more.

Avant-Garde

Experimental and unconventional. This style pushes boundaries and challenges traditional fashion norms with innovative and sometimes abstract designs.

Formal/Business

Professional and polished, including suits, dress shirts, and formal shoes. It's often associated with office wear and important events.

Athleisure

Combines athletic wear with casual style, like yoga pants, leggings, and sporty tops that can be worn both for exercise and everyday activities.

Eclectic

A mix-and-match style that combines various elements from different styles, creating a unique and often personalized look.
